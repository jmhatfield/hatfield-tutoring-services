Hatfield Tutoring Services, LLC.

Private tutoring in reading, writing, elementary math, and organizational coaching

Services

Hatfield Tutoring Services, LLC. provides individually tailored, private tutoring instruction in reading, writing, math, and organizational coaching to preschool and elementary school students.

Marie Hatfield specializes in teaching students with dyslexia and other learning differences. She is trained in the Orton-Gillingham instructional approach and the Visualizing and Verbalizing® program.

Marie uses multi-sensory techniques to make learning fun and stress-free. Students' improvements are tracked by ongoing assessments, and parents can expect to receive consistent communication on their child's progress.

Availability & Rates

In-person and virtual tutoring appointments are available Monday through Saturday. Rates are determined on a client-by-client basis. For more information, contact Marie.

About Marie

Marie E. Hatfield M.S. Ed.

Maryland State Department of Education (MSDE) certified

Marie Hatfield has been teaching for over 25 years, most recently as a private school resource teacher, where she founded the school's resource program. Prior to working in private schools, she worked as a tutor at Bowman Educational Services.

Marie has dedicated her life to helping children with learning differences, and she's a strong advocate of early intervention. Her philosophy is to make progress as quickly as possible, while honoring that each child learns at a different pace.
