About Marie

Credentials

Marie Hatfield has been teaching for over 25 years, both in schools and as a private tutor. She has dedicated her life to helping children overcome learning challenges, and she's a strong advocate of early intervention and building solid foundations. Marie uses multi-sensory techniques to make learning fun and stress-free.
